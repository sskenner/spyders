As the Security Analyst , you will ensure Blend's compliance with security commitments and best practices. You will lead, develop, implement or manage on-going controls to meet security standards such as ISO 27001 and SOC 2 control objectives. You will be responsible for testing, documenting, evaluating, and remediating internal controls and collaborating with internal and external audit teams, IT management, and other stakeholders to ensure compliance project deliverables are met.

Responsibilities

    • Conduct risk assessments on business and operational technologies, processes, procedures, and policies
    • Evaluate the information security posture of vendors and partners
    • Speak authoritatively about information security controls and techniques used to protect customer and consumer interests
    • Coordinate and inform security certification auditors and customer auditors about Blend's security and threat model
    • Design and develop security processes and controls to protect the confidentiality, integrity and availability of the company’s assets
    • Provide Blend teams with guidance around security policy implementation, execution, and monitoring
    • Create and administer security training programs for Blend teams across business and technical functions
    • Review customer and partner contracts for compliance implications

Requirements

    • Technical competence demonstrated with experience as a system administrator or software developer
    • BA/BS or 4+ relevant experience
    • At least 4 years of internal information security experience implementing and managing security controls and policy
    • Strong project management skills
    • Exposure to cloud-based technologies such as AWS, GitHub and JIRA

Nice to haves

    • Prior work experience as an IT auditor
    • Certified Information Systems Auditor (CISA) or Certified Information Systems Security Professional (CISSP) designations
    • Project Management Lite (PMBOK)
    • Hands on experiences in conducting SOC 2 and ISO 27001 audits in internal and external capacity.
    • Prior experience with governance and controls frameworks, such as COBIT, NIST, ITIL, ISO, NIST, FISMA, FedRAMP, PCI DSS, HIPAA or HITRUST
    • Vendor management experience
